COMMUNITY

ZERO FOODPRINT

Emmer and Rye is a carbon neutral restaurant. We partner with Zero Foodprint to help farmers implement carbon farming (also called regenerative farming) projects through grants from their Restore Programs.

Want to learn more about Carbon Farming? Visit Zero Foodprint

BRISTOL-JOSEPH CULINARY SCHOLARSHIP

In 2020, working with the Austin Community College (ACC) and the Greater Austin Black Chamber of Commerce, we have raised funds for a scholarship. One to two students will be awarded a full scholarship to the Pastry or Culinary Program at ACC along with mentorship for the year.

Interested in donating? Visit the Greater Austin Black Chamber of Commerce Site and select the Bristol-Joseph Scholarship Fund

AISD PROGRAM AT HENBIT

During COVID we were honored for Henbit to be selected to help provide meals for caregivers through the Austin Independent School District. We were able to serve over 400,000 caregivers.
