JUNETEENTH (PERCENTAGE NIGHT)

Dine at any of our restaurants on June 19, and we'll donate the proceeds to the Neill-Cochran House Museum. The Neill-Cochran House Museum was home to the Asylum for the Blind for a few years after the estate’s completion in 1856. Still hidden within its grandeur is the story of those whose hands built it (commonly credited only to architect Abner Cook), and those tapped to serve its white residents.
